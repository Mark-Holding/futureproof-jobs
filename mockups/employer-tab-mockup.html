Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between per-post and subscription pricing?

Per-post pricing is ideal for organizations with occasional hiring needs, while subscription plans are more cost-effective for companies with regular recruitment activities. Subscriptions also include additional features like resume database access and advanced analytics.

Can I upgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade your subscription plan at any time. The price difference will be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrading is also possible at the end of your current billing period.

How do resume database searches work?

Resume database searches allow you to proactively search our talent pool of candidates with verified human skills. You can filter by specific capabilities, AI Resistance Index scores, industry experience, and more. Each search counts as one credit from your monthly allowance.

What is the AI Resistance Index verification?

Our proprietary AI Resistance Index measures each candidate's skills and capabilities against current and projected automation technology. The verification process ensures that candidates possess the human skills they claim and quantifies their resilience to automation in your specific industry.
